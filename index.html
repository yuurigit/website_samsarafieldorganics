About Samsara Fields Organics

Welcome to Samsara Fields Organics, located in Waterford, Ontario, Canada. Our mission is deeply rooted in sustainability, organic farming, and preserving heirloom produce.


At the 2022 Toronto Garlic Festival, we showcased a diverse array of offerings, from mixed vegetables like tomatoes, peppers, eggplant, and lettuce, to a rich variety of garlic strains such as Elephant, Ted 42, and Rose de Lautrec.


In addition to our fresh produce, we take pride in our range of artisanal products, including canned garlic scapes, seasonal preserves, and our prized Black Garlic. Our commitment to innovation extends to our fermented garlic and scapes, as well as our selection of freeze-dried fruits and vegetables, chips, and tortillas.


Samsara Fields is more than just a farm; it's a testament to our family's dedication to environmental stewardship and global consciousness. Inspired by the challenges of food shortages, environmental degradation, and social unrest, we found solace and purpose in organic vegetable production.


Our journey began as a humble effort to preserve our farm and has evolved into a beacon of hope and sustainability. Through our products, we offer not only nourishment but also a connection to the land and a sense of community. We are proud to collaborate with partners like Maizal Tortilleria, uniting our passion for quality and ethical sourcing.

Join us on our mission to cultivate healing and love through organic farming, one harvest at a time.
